MONKEYS

The dusky leaf monkey, also known as the spectacled langur or spectacled leaf monkey (Trachypithecus obscurus), is a species of primate in the family Cercopithecidae. It is found in Malaysia, Burma, and Thailand.

These monkeys are known for the white circles around their eyes, giving them a "spectacled" appearance. They are mostly folivorous, eating a diet rich in leaves and fruits, and are known for their gentle and social nature.

Dusky leaf monkeys usually live in small groups and spend most of their time in trees. They are an important species in their ecosystems for seed dispersal and forest regeneration.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

You will find the monkeys at the monkey cage near the Monkey Trail.
